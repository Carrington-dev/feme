Welcome to the F.A.N Club

The increasing complexity, government red-tapes and bureaucracy that have made it quite difficult for many business women to thrive and successfully operate businesses in South Africa, has affected the entrepreneurial sector and spirit immensely due to the lack of compliance and access to information that many women who run businesses in South Africa are faced with

Our urgent response

In response, we have established the Femme Achievers Network, which is a Non-Profit Organization that aims to facilitate a platform that will assist business women in getting their businesses and institutions to be compliant. This platform is beyond an NPO,

The platform

It is a home that aims to create a safe space for business women by exposing them to various opportunities such as assuring your company is compliant according to the service or product compliance requirements, funding, networking, skills, training and a mentorship

About Us

Femme Achievers Network was established as a concept that functioned as a business consulting firm in 2018. The concept was established by our founder and Chairwoman Karabo, who has business extensive knowledge in business compliance. Through her consulting firm, Karabo discovered that there has been quite a lot of women who operate businesses on an informal basis and are denied access to various opportunities due to lack of compliance

These lack of compliance can vary from failing to update CIPC with annual returns resulting in company deregistration, registering their businesses, failing to have a tax clearance, a business account, a BEE certificate or TAX registration. These requirements have put many women in quite vulnerable positions where they are taken advantaged of and exploited since they lack access to such valuable information

In response to this Karabo decided that a platform like this will protect women in the business space and will act as a vanguard for them to gain access to various information and opportunities that will allow them to operate their businesses with ease
